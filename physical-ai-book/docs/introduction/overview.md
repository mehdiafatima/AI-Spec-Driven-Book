# Book Overview: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This book is designed to provide students and enthusiasts with a deep understanding of the theoretical concepts, practical implementations, and cutting-edge applications in the field.

## Course Structure

This textbook is structured around a 13-week course, covering a wide range of topics from foundational robotics principles to advanced AI integration with humanoid platforms. Each module includes theory, lab exercises, and assessments to ensure a holistic learning experience.

## What You Will Learn

-   **Foundational Robotics**: Master the basics of Robot Operating System 2 (ROS 2), robot modeling with URDF, and simulation environments like Gazebo.
-   **Digital Twins**: Explore the concept and implementation of digital twins for robotics, enabling robust simulation and testing.
-   **NVIDIA Isaac Sim**: Dive into advanced simulation and AI-powered robotics development using NVIDIA Isaac Sim.
-   **VLA & Humanoids**: Understand Visual Language Models (VLA) and their critical role in enabling complex interactions for humanoid robots.
-   **Real-World Applications**: Ground theoretical knowledge in practical, real-world examples that are executable on accessible hardware (Jetson, RTX workstations) or cloud platforms.

## Who This Book Is For

This book is ideal for:
-   Undergraduate and graduate students in robotics, AI, computer science, and engineering.
-   Researchers and developers looking to expand their knowledge in physical AI and humanoid robotics.
-   Anyone with a keen interest in the future of intelligent robots.

## Prerequisites

A basic understanding of programming (Python is preferred), linear algebra, and calculus will be beneficial. Familiarity with Linux environments is also recommended.
